City of Cardiff Symphony Orchestra

Welcome to CCSO's website.

CCSO is an amateur orchestra based in Cardiff. We perform at least five concerts per year with varied, challenging programmes, often with high-class, professional soloists.

Our next concert is on Saturday 19th November 2016 at 7.30 p.m. and the programme will be:

Elgar: Polonia
Szymanowski: Violin Concerto No. 1
Elgar: Symphony No. 1

For an email reminder for each of our concerts, please contact us.
