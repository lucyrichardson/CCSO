    The next orchestral concert performed by the City of Cardiff Symphony Orchestra will be held on:

    Saturday 28th October 2023 at 8.00 p.m.

    in St Martin's Church, Roath

    The programme will be:
    Wagner: Concert performance of Act 1 of Die Walküre

    The vocal soloists will be Fiona Harrison-Wolfe (Sieglinde), Gareth Dafydd Morris (Siegmund) and James Platt (Hunding), all of whom are from the Welsh National Opera. Tickets cost £10 or £8 for concessions and will be available at the door.
