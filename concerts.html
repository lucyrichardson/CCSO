    The next orchestral concert performed by the City of Cardiff Symphony Orchestra will be held on:

    Tuesday 12th April 2022 at 7.30 p.m.

    in All Saints Church, Barry CF62 3NU

    The programme will be:

    Suk: Schezo Fantastique
    Mozart: Bassoon Concerto
    Dvorak: Symphony No. 9 (New World)

    The soloist in the bassoon concerto will be Steve Marsden, principal bassoonist in the Orchestra of the Welsh National Opera. The concert will be given as part of the Barry Arts Festival. Tickets cost £5 - £11.
