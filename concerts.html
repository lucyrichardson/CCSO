    The next orchestral concert performed by the City of Cardiff Symphony Orchestra will be held on:

    Friday 9th February 2024 at 8.00 p.m.

    in Stanwell Road Baptist Church, Penarth

    The programme will be:
    Fauré: Masques et bergamasques
    Copland: Clarinet Concerto
    Nielsen: Symphony No. 2 (The Four Temperaments)

    The soloist in the clarinet concerto will be Thomas Verity from the orchestra of the Welsh National Opera. Tickets cost £10 or £8 for concessions and will be available at the door.
