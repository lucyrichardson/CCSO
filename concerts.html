    The next orchestral concert performed by the City of Cardiff Symphony Orchestra will be held on:

    Thursday 24th February 2022 at 8.00 p.m.

    in St. John the Evangelist Church, Canton, Cardiff CF5 1NX

    The programme will be:

    Grieg: Peer Gynt Suite No. 1
    Haydn: Trumpet Concerto
    Nielsen: Symphony No. 4 (The Inextinguishable)

    The soloist in the trumpet concerto will be Dean Wright, principal trumpet in the Orchestra of the Welsh National Opera.
