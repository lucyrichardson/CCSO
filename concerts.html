    The next orchestral concert performed by the City of Cardiff Symphony Orchestra will be held on:

    Tuesday 29th April 2025 at 7.00 p.m.

    in the University Concert Hall, Corbett Road, Cardiff

    The programme will be:
    Augusta Holmès: Andromède
    Maurice Ravel: Piano Concerto in G
    César Franck: Symphony in D minor

    The soloist in the piano concerto will be Catherine Milledge. Ticket prices are £10 or £8 for concessions and will be available at the door.
