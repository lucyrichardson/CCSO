    The next orchestral concert performed by the City of Cardiff Symphony Orchestra will be held on:

    Friday 27th April 2018 at 8.00 p.m.

    in St. John's Church, Canton

    The programme for the concert will be:

    Tchaikovsky: Romeo and Juliet overture
    Rimsky-Korsakov: Trombone Concerto
    Mussorgsky: Pictures at an Exhibition

    The soloist in the trombone concerto will be Roger Cutts, who is principal trombonist in the orchestra of the Welsh National Opera.
