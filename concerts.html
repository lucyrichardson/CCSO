    The next orchestral concert performed by the City of Cardiff Symphony Orchestra will be held on:

    Sunday 30th April 2023 at 4.00 p.m.

    in St John the Evangelist Church, Canton

    The programme will be:

    Kaprálová: Suita Rustica
    Mozart: Bassoon Concerto
    Dvořák: Symphony No. 7

    The soloist in the bassoon concerto will be Steve Marsden, who is principal bassoon in the Orchestra of the Welsh National Opera. Tickets cost £10 or £8 for concessions and will be available at the door.
