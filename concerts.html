    The next concert performed by the City of Cardiff Symphony Orchestra will be held on:

    Sunday 9th November 2025 at 7.30 p.m.

    in the Church of St. John the Evangelist, Canton CF5 1NX

    The programme will be:
    Butterworth: A Shropshire Lad and Banks of Green Willow
    Mozart: Sinfonia Concertante for Violin, Viola and orchestra
    Vaughan Williams: Symphony No.6

    The soloists in the Sinfonia Concertante will be Ruth Henry (violin) and Laura Sinnerton (viola). Ticket prices are £12 (or £10 for concessions) and will be available at the door.
