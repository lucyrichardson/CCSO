    The next orchestral concert performed by the City of Cardiff Symphony Orchestra will be held on:

    Wednesday 31st October 2018 at 8.00 p.m.

    in All Saints Church, Penarth

    The programme for the concert will be:

    Glazunov: Autumn
    Mozart: Sinfonia Concertante for Violin, Viola and Orchestra
    Tchaikovsky: Sympnony No. 4

    The soloists in Sinfonia Concertante will be Lucy Gould (violin) and David Adams (viola). Lucy Gould plays violin in the Gould Trio and David Adams is leader of the Orchestra of the Welsh National Opera.
